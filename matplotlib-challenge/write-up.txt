Observations

1. Over the 45 day treatment Capomulin proved to be the most effect drug and the only one that reduced the size of the tumor.

2. Mice who were treated with Capomulin had over an 80% survival after 40 days while every other drug had less than 60% survival rate after 40 days.

3. Capomulin proved most effective in reducing metastatic spread with around 1.5 metastatic sites after 40 days while Ketapril proved least effective with over 3 metastatic sites after 40 days of treatment.
